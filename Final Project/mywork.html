Form and Function Merch Design

Form and function is a student-run, pro-bono, marketing agency that I am an executive member of and I was entrusted with designing the merch for the next school year. This design is one of many I created to be printed on the back of a black hoodie and worn proudly by our members. While creating this design, I kept in mind what Form and Function stands for: our ability to mold and adapt to whatever is handed our way. Thus the logo looks stretched, shaped and formed, but resilient nonetheless.
